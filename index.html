About the Game

Hold on tight to your balloons and take to the skies! In Poultry Pop, you play as a daring adventurer floating through the air, held aloft by colorful balloons. But watch out - mischievous birds are out to pop your balloons and send you tumbling!

Armed with your trusty yoyo, fend off waves of feathered foes as you float higher and higher. How long can you stay airborne?
